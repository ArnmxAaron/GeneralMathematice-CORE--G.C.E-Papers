Question 11: Geometry & GP
(a) Triangle Area Calculation: Given: An equilateral/isosceles triangle with angle 60°. Height calculation: sin 60° = h / x h = x sin 60° Area of Triangle = 1/2 × base × height 144√3 = 1/2 × x × (x sin 60°) 144√3 = 1/2 × x² × (√3 / 2) 144√3 = (x²√3) / 4 x² = 144 × 4 = 576 x = √576 x = 24 cm Perimeter = 3x (assuming equilateral as per context) Perimeter = 3 × 24 = 72 cm (b) Geometric Progression (GP): Sequence: 144, x, 64 Common Ratio (r) = Second/First = Third/Second x / 144 = 64 / x x × x = 144 × 64 x² = 9216 x = √9216 x = 96
Question 12: Coordinate Geometry & Statistics
(a)(i) Find x given P(x, -9), Q(4, 26) and gradient m = 5. Gradient = (y₂ - y₁) / (x₂ - x₁) 5 = (26 - (-9)) / (4 - x) 5 = (26 + 9) / (4 - x) 5 = 35 / (4 - x) 5(4 - x) = 35 20 - 5x = 35 -5x = 35 - 20 -5x = 15 x = -3 (a)(ii) Equation of the line: Using y - y₁ = m(x - x₁) with point (-3, -9) y - (-9) = 5(x - (-3)) y + 9 = 5(x + 3) y + 9 = 5x + 15 y = 5x + 15 - 9 y = 5x + 6 (b) Statistics (Mean): Table: x: 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 f: 4, 10, 13, 8, x fx: 24, 70, 104, 72, 10x Mean = Σfx / Σf = 8 (270 + 10x) / (35 + x) = 8 270 + 10x = 8(35 + x) 270 + 10x = 280 + 8x 10x - 8x = 280 - 270 2x = 10 x = 5

Question 8: Probability
