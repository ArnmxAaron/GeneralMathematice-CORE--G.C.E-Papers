Question 12: Coordinate Geometry & Statistics
(a)(i) Find x given P(x, -9), Q(4, 26) and gradient m = 5. Gradient = (y₂ - y₁) / (x₂ - x₁) 5 = (26 - (-9)) / (4 - x) 5 = (26 + 9) / (4 - x) 5 = 35 / (4 - x) 5(4 - x) = 35 20 - 5x = 35 -5x = 35 - 20 -5x = 15 x = -3 (a)(ii) Equation of the line: Using y - y₁ = m(x - x₁) with point (-3, -9) y - (-9) = 5(x - (-3)) y + 9 = 5(x + 3) y + 9 = 5x + 15 y = 5x + 15 - 9 y = 5x + 6 (b) Statistics (Mean): Table: x: 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 f: 4, 10, 13, 8, x fx: 24, 70, 104, 72, 10x Mean = Σfx / Σf = 8 (270 + 10x) / (35 + x) = 8 270 + 10x = 8(35 + x) 270 + 10x = 280 + 8x 10x - 8x = 280 - 270 2x = 10 x = 5

Question 2: Work Word Problem
Let Mr. Kubi take y days. Mr. Adu takes y - 10 days. Together they take 12 days. Equation: 1/y + 1/(y-10) = 1/12 [ (y - 10) + y ] / [ y(y - 10) ] = 1/12 (2y - 10) / (y² - 10y) = 1/12 12(2y - 10) = y² - 10y 24y - 120 = y² - 10y y² - 34y + 120 = 0 Using Quadratic Formula: y = [-(-34) ± √((-34)² - 4(1)(120))] / 2(1) y = [34 ± √(1156 - 480)] / 2 y = [34 ± √676] / 2 y = [34 ± 26] / 2 y = (34 + 26)/2 or y = (34 - 26)/2 y = 60/2 = 30 or y = 8/2 = 4 Since Adu takes y-10, y must be > 10. So y = 30. Mr. Kubi takes 30 days. Mr. Adu takes 20 days.
